The Company

NovoTech is a Norwegian software company and a technology leader in 3D visualization. The NovoRender cloud allows engineering teams to share their advanced 3D-models with employees, customers and business partners, regardless of 3D formats and file sizes. By making 3D engineering drawings available to anyone at anytime, our customers can dramatically increase the value of existing 3D models during the development of large projects.

The Technology

The NovoRender cloud makes it possible to visualize endless amounts of 3D data using any browser on any device. First, NovoRender combines 3D models in different formats to create the full scene. Next, NovoRender splits the combined 3D model into small chunks. Finally, the small chunks are streamed on demand to the viewer, dependent on which part of the scene is being displayed.

This is different from the traditional way of viewing 3D models which is based on sending the entire 3D file to the viewer before rendering the scene. By splitting large models into small chunks and stremaing them on an as-needed basis, any 3D model can be visualized on any device with a web-browser. In addition, access to metadata is enabled, allowing the user to click on and inspect any element in the scene. No plugins or downloads are required.
