NovoTech

NovoTech is a technology leader in 3D visualization. Our unique technology allows engineering firms to share their advanced 3D-models with employees, customers and business partners, dramatically increasing the value of existing 3D models during the development of large projects.

Technology

The NovoRender cloud makes is possible to use and visialize endless amounts of 3D data. By splitting large models into small chunks and stremaing them on an as-needed basis, any 3D model can be visualized on any device with a web-browser. No plugins or downloads are required.
